Not feeling it? Your secret is safe with Flaked.

We get it. You’ve been waiting for this one-on-one all week, but when the time comes, you just aren’t feeling it. Life happens, let Flaked help ease the tension of cancelling.

How it works

Schedule Meetings

Through the Flaked app, you can schedule meetings with the press of a button or sync with your favorite calendar app.

Share Meetings Seamlessly

Quickly sync up meeting invites using email invites or QR code scan.

Secretly Cancel

Not feeling it? Mark yourself as willing not to meet. Your secret is safe with Flaked.

Meeting Halfway

If both attendees tap out, Flaked marks the event as cancelled.

Instant Notification

Flaked will send you a push notification letting you know it’s okay. The meeting is off.

Discretion By Default

In case the meeting goes through, Flaked will protect your privacy. No one needs to know.
